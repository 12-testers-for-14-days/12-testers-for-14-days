Common Questions

Why do I need 12 testers?

Since November 2023, Google requires personal developer accounts to run a closed test with at least 20 testers for 14 days. If you already have 8 friends, our 12 testers fill the gap to ensure you meet the quota safely.

What happens if I get rejected?

It's rare with our service (less than 0.2%). However, if Google rejects the production access request, we will restart the testing cycle for free or provide a full refund. We are in this together.

Are these bots?

Absolutely not. We manage a community of real Android users (students, tech enthusiasts) who install apps for rewards. Google's algorithms can detect bots; our human testers ensure genuine engagement signals.
