    FAQ

    Questions? Answered.

    Still have questions? Chat with us
    Are these real humans or automated bots?
    100% real human testers on physical Android devices. We never use bots, scripts, or emulators. Google's systems are sophisticated and detect automation — our 99.9% approval rate exists because we don't compromise on this. You can verify device models in your Play Console dashboard.
    What if Google rejects my production access?
    If rejected due to the testing phase not meeting requirements, we repeat the full 14-day cycle at zero additional cost. If it fails a second time, you receive a 100% refund with no questions asked. Note: refunds don't cover rejections caused by your app violating Google's content or policy rules (unrelated to testing).
    How fast does testing start?
    Most orders have testers assigned within 12–24 hours of payment. In many cases, testers appear in your Play Console the same day you order. You'll receive an email confirmation once testing has started.
    Do you need my APK or developer account login?
    No. You only need to share your app's package name or internal testing track link from Play Console. You add our testers' email addresses — we never access your developer account or need your credentials. Your account stays 100% in your control.
    Do testers actually use the app every day?
    Yes, and this is critical. Google tracks active session data, not just install counts. Our testers are briefed to open and meaningfully engage with your app daily for all 14 days. You'll see session data confirming this directly in your Play Console dashboard.
    Does this work for any type of app?
    Yes — games, utilities, productivity tools, social apps, e-commerce, tools, and more. The only exceptions are apps requiring specialized hardware or apps violating Google Play policies. If you're unsure, chat with us on WhatsApp before ordering.
    Can I test 3 or more apps at the same time?
    Absolutely. The Agency plan handles 3 or more apps at $15 each. Testing can run in parallel or on a staggered schedule — your dedicated account manager coordinates everything and keeps you updated throughout.
    How do I pay and is it secure?
    Payments are processed exclusively via Payoneer — the same platform used by Amazon, Shopify, and Airbnb. Your card details are encrypted end-to-end and never stored by us. The full checkout is HTTPS/SSL secured.
    How do I track testing progress?
    You can monitor everything in your Google Play Console — tester count, device models, installs, and session data in real time. Our support team is also available 24/7 via WhatsApp and email if you have questions during the testing period.
    What happens after 14 days complete?
    We send you a clear, step-by-step guide for submitting your production access request to Google. Most approvals arrive within 3–7 business days. Testers remain in your testing group unless you remove them. Then — you publish your app to the world.
    Is this compliant with Google's terms of service?
    Yes. Google's policy specifically requires real human testers — which is precisely what we provide. We don't circumvent any rules; we fulfill them legitimately. Over 3,500 approved apps and zero account bans confirm our compliance record.
    Can testers provide custom feedback about my app?
    Yes. Testers submit structured feedback through Play Console's built-in system. If you want them to focus on specific features or flows, you can include notes when ordering. Our testers are experienced across all app categories and can provide focused, useful feedback on request.
